Ireland is home to over 7,000 pubs, according to a new study. A report by the Drinks Industry Group of Ireland (DIGI) , which was authored by DCU Economist Anthony Foley, states that Ireland has 7,193 pubs, as well as 3,161 off-licenses, 2,406 restaurants, and 983 hotels. The report aims to examine “the economic, social and cultural contribution of the drinks and hospitality sector across Ireland,” and how that sector plays a vital role in rural communities and contributes to local economies throughout Ireland.
